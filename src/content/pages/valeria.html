Valeria

My name is Valeria Colella. I am an editor specialized in digital publications and proofreader of legal texts. With studies in Modern Literature, Design and Journalism, a postgraduate degree in Management of Creative Enterprises (UNC) and a diploma in Arts and Culture Management (UNC Graduate Foundation), and more than 20 years of experience as a proofreader, editor and institutional communicator for companies and academic organizations. I have worked in the creative direction of e-Literalia and Verba Volant, Scripta Manent. I currently coordinate the projects of VaCo Publicaciones Digitales and Corrector Jurídico, a publishing services company aimed at professional individuals and companies in the legal field. I also do mentoring on Digital Publishing and Digital Narratives in workshops of Municipalidad de Córdoba, Escuela Superior de Artes Aplicadas "Lino Enea Spilimbergo" and Escuela Superior de Bellas Artes "Dr. José Figueroa Alcorta". I teach legal discourse and plain language at USAL and I am a founding member of PLECA, Profesionales de la Lengua Española Correcta de la Argentina.
