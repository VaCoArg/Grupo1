About

ES
We are DHW, a team of digital humanists working from their areas and disciplines (history, communication and literature), in a digital project. This project consists of the historical and communicational analysis of a very important event for the history of humanity, such as the secret protocols of the non-aggression pact between the USSR and Germany, better known as the Ribbentrop-Molov Pact, during World War II.
